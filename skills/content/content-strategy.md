# Content strategy

## Overview
The layer above individual posts: plan what to make, turn one idea into a week of content,
write scroll-stopping hooks, and improve discoverability.

## When to use
"Make a content calendar", "repurpose this into…", "give me hooks for…", "what hashtags
should I use", "help my post rank / get found".

## Workflow
- **Calendar**: pick cadence and pillars (e.g. teach / build-in-public / proof / offer);
  lay out a 1–4 week schedule (date · platform · pillar · idea · CTA) as a table.
- **Repurpose**: take one asset (doc, video, long post) and spin it into a thread, a
  carousel outline, a short-form script, a newsletter blurb, and 3 captions.
- **Hooks**: produce 8–10 hook variants (curiosity, contrarian, result, question, story).
- **Hashtags / SEO**: suggest a mix of broad + niche tags; for SEO, give a title, meta
  description, and the primary keyword used naturally.

## Output format
- Calendars as Markdown tables.
- Repurposing as a labeled list of derived pieces.
- Saved to `documents/<slug>.md`.

## Quality bar
Plan is realistic for one person; pillars are distinct; hooks are specific (not generic
"Did you know"); hashtag/SEO advice is honest, not spammy.

## Examples
- "Plan a week of content for my course." → 7-row table across platforms with CTAs.
- "Repurpose this report into social." → thread + carousel + short script + 3 captions.
